# Synapse

Autonomous Google Business Profile Growth Agent

## Vision

Synapse is an autonomous AI agent designed to operate, optimize, and grow a Google Business Profile (GBP) with minimal human intervention.

Its primary objective is to continuously improve local search visibility, customer engagement, review sentiment, profile completeness, content freshness, and business discovery while aligning with the business owner's strategic goals.

The first deployment target is:

**Eritage ENT Care – Entebbe**

However, the architecture is designed to support multiple businesses in the future.

---

## Mission

Transform a Google Business Profile from a passive listing into an actively managed growth engine.

Synapse continuously:

- Monitors profile performance
- Identifies growth opportunities
- Creates and publishes content
- Responds to customer interactions
- Tracks visibility metrics
- Generates recommendations
- Executes approved actions
- Learns from historical performance

---

## Core Philosophy

Most businesses update their Google Business Profile sporadically.

Synapse treats a GBP as a living digital asset that requires continuous optimization.

Instead of reacting to problems, Synapse proactively:

- Detects opportunities
- Executes growth actions
- Measures outcomes
- Refines future strategies

The long-term goal is autonomous business profile management.
